Dr. Adam DJ Brett is Editor-in-Chief of [*Ebenezer: A Journal of Public Theology & Public Witness*](/). He serves with the [American Indian Law Alliance](https://aila.ngo), supporting Indigenous-led advocacy, research, and public education. He also teaches at United Lutheran Seminary, where his work connects public theology, Indigenous studies, and digital humanities. His digital humanities projects build public-facing archives and storytelling platforms that document histories of domination and support community-centered scholarship through podcasts, websites, and open-access publishing.
